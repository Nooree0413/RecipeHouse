Caprese Salad with Pesto Sauce

Posted By : Admin | Date : 27-03-2015


A great antipasto bite to start your meal with.
Juicy tomatoes and mozzarella cheese salad topped with freshly made pesto sauce.

INGREDIENTS:

2 Tomatoes
Handful of Tulsi
1 packet Buffalo Mozzarella

For Pesto Sauce:

Handful of Basil
1 cup Pine nuts
1 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
Juice of half a Lemon

ADDITIONAL INFO
Yield 2 Servings
Prep Time15 Minutes
Cook Time 30 minutes
Total Time45 minutes

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Cut 2 juicy tomatoes in thick rounds and dress them with a little bit of extra virgin olive oil, some salt and pepper.
  2. Break Mozzarella into chunks.
  3. On a serving platter, arrange the tomato slices along with the mozzarella chunks.
  4. Garnish with tulsi and pesto sauce.
  5. Pesto Sauce:
  6. Blend the basil with pine nuts, Parmesan cheese with extra virgin olive oil, until it forms a paste.
  7. Squeeze in the lemon juice.
  8. If the paste is too thick, add in more extra virgin olive oil to reach the desired consistency
